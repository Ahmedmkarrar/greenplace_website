About Us

Our story

Our Story

Greenplace is natural art that preserves the feeling of the forest in your interior for many years.

The brand's story began in 2016, when I first discovered decor made from preserved plants. The idea of maintaining natural beauty without maintenance was so powerful that what started as a passion grew into a craft — and then into a full workshop.

We studied botany, preservation technologies, and global practices, refining our own process over the years. Our goal is to achieve a natural look, softness, and long-lasting quality while maintaining environmentally responsible materials.

Each piece is created by hand. It is not just decor — it is an atmosphere, a sense of calm, and the feeling of "living" nature inside a home or space.

Today, we continue to grow our workshop in the United States, creating unique moss art and natural compositions for private interiors and commercial projects.

Nature should be close — even in the city.

What Our Pieces Are Made Of

Our artworks are created from preserved moss, plants, and lichens. The frames are made from natural wood — pine, cedar, or oak. Adjustable LED backlighting can be added upon request.

What Is Preserved Moss?

It is real forest moss that has undergone a special treatment in which its natural moisture is replaced with a safe, glycerin-based solution.

Because of this, it retains its softness, rich color, and fresh appearance for many years — without watering, light, or maintenance.

Unlike ordinary dried moss, preserved moss does not crumble or fade.

We create natural compositions that keep the feeling of the forest in your interior for years.
